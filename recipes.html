Steaming Mocha Cocoa
  • 2 cups of milk
  • 2 tablespoons of cocoa
  • 2 tablespoons of brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon of ground coffee
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  1. Heat all ingredients in a small sauce pan
  2. Whisk until steaming
  3. Strain and pour
Hot Buttered Toffee Coffee
  • 4 onces of mocha ice cream
  • 6 ounces of hot coffee
  • 2 tablespoons butterscotch topping
  • 2 teaspoons almond liqueur
  • whipped topping
  • 1 tablespoon of toffee pieces
  1. Spoon ice cream into a large coffee mug
  2. Pour coffee, butterscotch toppping and almond lqueur over ice cream
  3. Garnish with a doop of whipped cream and sprink toffee chips
Creamy Iced Vanilla Caramel Coffee
  • 4 cups of brewed coffee
  • 1 cup of milk
  • 1/3 cup of french vanilla non-dairy coffee creamer
  • 1⁄4 cup caramel ice cream topping
  • 3 cups crushed ice
  • canned whipped cream (optional)
  1. Place all ingredients into blender and blend on high until the ice is completely smooth
  2. Pour
  3. Top with a dollop of whipped cream
Gingerbread Coffee
  • 3 tablespoons coffee beans, coarse ground
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1⁄4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1⁄8 teaspoon ground allspice
  • 1⁄2 teaspoon ground ginger
  • 1 tablespoon molasses
  • Boiling water
  • Hot milk
  1. Put all ingredients, except water and milk, into a 12 oz. press pot
  2. Add boiling water
  3. Stir with chopstick or handle of wooden spoon
  4. Put lid on pot and brew 4 minutes
  5. Press plunger and pour coffee into large cup
  6. Add hot milk to taste
